Volunteer With Us

We are teaching a group of sixteen students at Metro Business Academy how to code. Once a week, we go into class at MBA and show them how to do cool things with a text editor. We move at a fast, efficient pace. On the fourth day of class, all sixteen students successfully displayed a rotating, 3-dimensional, colored cube in the center of their web browser.

Our student:teacher ratio is 4:1. Help us improve it!

Teach Practical Skills

We have a limited amount of time to teach the students, so we emphasize efficient teaching. We know that our curriculum alone cannot teach them how to code. Therefore, we spend our time in class keeping them motivated to learn on their own, and confident they can. We write tutorials for students every week, and maintain a Piazza classroom for them to ask questions.

One student started learning Python without any help!

Improve Yourself

If you can't teach it, you don't know it... but if you don't know it, the best way to learn it is to teach it! No matter what your skill level, you will benefit from teaching your knowledge and giving back to the community. You will solidify your programming knowledge by writing tutorials, teaching class, and answering questions. This is a great opportunity to learn more about yourself.
